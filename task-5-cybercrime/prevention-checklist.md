# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Always verify the sender’s email address before responding
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details
- Check website URLs carefully before logging in
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Keep your devices and apps updated

## UPI / Financial Safety
- Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
- Double-check UPI ID or phone number before sending money

## Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
